Project Overview

Purpose, Intended Users, Overview

The website will provide information about the Mint Hill Chess Club in order to advertise it and store pertinent information for those who have already attended. The intended users are those interested in joining a chess club and those who have already attended our club. The website will contain information about the club, give an introduction to the game, provide event information, display a gallery of pictures of the club, and track tournament results.

Page Designs

  • Home Page
    • Purpose: Advertise the club to people who have not yet joined, emphasizing that the club is meant for immediate players.
    • Audience: People who have not yet joined our club but are interested.
    • Content: Give description of the club, location, and organizers. There's no hyperlinks or buttons, but there's an embedded map so users can better understand the location of the club. User does not enter any data.
  • Events
    • Purpose: Educate people about past and future events, including whether the event is casual or competitive.
    • Audience: Those interested in joining the club and those already attending the club.
    • Content: Lists all previous and upcoming events in a visually appealing way, describing the date, time, and type of event. Users may click a button to RSVP to an upcoming event, which will link to the main MeetUp page.
  • Photo Gallery
    • Purpose: Advertise the club to people who have not yet joined, making it seem fun, community-oriented, and generally casual. Also allows current attendees to look back at positive memories of the club..
    • Audience: People interested in joining the club and those currently attending.
    • Content: Display a photo gallery of pictures taken at the club. Users may click on an image in order to see it in flil screen and then click left or right arrow buttons to navigate between images. There's no further buttons or hyperlinks. The user doesn't input any data.
  • How to Play
    • Purpose: Increase the chance of newcomers joining the club by giving them a basic overview of how to play the game, making them more confident and productive during the meeting.
    • Audience: People interested in joining the club who are not familiar with how to play chess.
    • Content: Displays text and images which help educate the user on how to play chess. Images include a chess board and chess pieces. There's no button, hyperlinks, and the user doesn't input any data.
  • Contact Us
    • Purpose: Provide information on how to contact the Mint Hill Chess Club.
    • Audience: People who have inquiries about the club, such as clarifications on the event time or type of event.
    • Content: Contact form including name, email, phone number, subject, and message.
