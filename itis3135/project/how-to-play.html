How To Play Chess

The game of chess is played on an 8x8 board between two sides: white and black. Both sides take turns moving one of their pieces until one players wins by checkmating their opponent or the game is a draw. A player has been checkmated when their King is attacked by an enemy piece and there's no safe square it can move to.

The game features 6 types of pieces:

  • King: moves one square horizontally, vertically, or diagonally
  • Queen moves any amount of squares horizontally, vertically, or diagonally
  • Bishop: moves any amount of squares diagonally
  • Knight: moves in an L-shape
  • Rook: moves any amount of squares horizontally or vertically
  • Pawn: moves one square up the board (or two squares from the starting position)
